David's Career

"Sometimes you win, sometimes you learn"

Info

I created this website in my leisure time as a way to remind myself of my past table tennis career. Throughout my fourteen years of playing, I have been lucky to travel across the world to not only pursue my passion, but to also learn and absorb new experiences and knowledge from different people. I have been honoured to represent my province and country in countless national and international tournaments and today I continue to persue my table tennis career at school by representing The University of British Columbia in North American tournaments.
